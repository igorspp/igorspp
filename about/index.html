About me

I am a microbiologist specialized in the molecular ecology of microbial communities. I have a MSc degree in Cellular and Molecular Biology from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (Porto Alegre, Brazil), and a PhD in Sciences from the University of Liège (Liège, Belgium). I am currently a post-doctoral researcher at the Arctic Microbial Ecology group (University of Helsinki, Helsinki, Finland).
