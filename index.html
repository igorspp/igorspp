DR. IGOR S. PESSI

I am a microbiologist specialized in the molecular ecology of microbial communities, particularly in polar environments. I have a MSc degree in Cellular and Molecular Biology from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (Porto Alegre, Brazil), and a PhD in Sciences from the University of Liège (Liège, Belgium).
