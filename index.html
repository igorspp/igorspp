About me

I am a microbiologist specialized in molecular ecology of microbial communities. I have a MSc degree in Cellular and Molecular Biology from the Federal University of Rio Grande do Sul (UFRGS, Porto Alegre, Brazil), and a PhD in Sciences from the University of Liège (ULg, Liège, Belgium).
